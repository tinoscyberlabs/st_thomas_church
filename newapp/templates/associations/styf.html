San Thome Youth Front

San Thome Youth Front (STYF) from St Thomas Forane Church, Jallahalli has proudly served as a thriving church youth group for the past three decades. Committed to nurturing spiritual growth and fostering social development, STYF has been an integral part of the church community. Rooted in faith, fellowship, and service, STYF offers a diverse range of activities, including regular meetings, vibrant fests, and engaging community outreach programs. These fests serve as meaningful platforms for spiritual reflection, cultural exchange, and fostering lasting connections. With a mission to inspire and empower the youth, STYF continues to guide young individuals on their faith journey and encourage active participation within the church and the broader society.
