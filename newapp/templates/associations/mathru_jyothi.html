Mathru Jyothi 

San Thom Mathru Jyothi was founded in the year 1995. This is the platform for mothers in the church and has been working voluntarily and actively in the field of spiritual, social and cultural activities in the parish. They organize classes on family welfare, provide financial support for the education, marriage for the girls from financially week families, regular visit to orphanages etc. Another important activity is that to visit families who have lost their family member(s) and pray for them.

Above vision is possible only by the grace of the Mighty. We seek your sincere prayers and support. We also cordially invite all mothers of the church to be active members of Mathru Jyothi and help in the growth and welfare all the families, Organization and the Parish.
