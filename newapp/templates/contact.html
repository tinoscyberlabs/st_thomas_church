How to reach

The church is easily accessible by road from central part of the city, railway stations and Bangalore international airport. It lies on the periphery of the outer Ring road in Jalahalli village between BEL circle and Goruguntepalya (Tumkur Road). It is about 1 Kilometer from BEL circle towards Tumkur Road. The exact place is Jalahalli Village, M.E.S. ring road, 5th Cross, Sharadamba Nagar.
Road:
The Bangalore Metropolitan Transport Corporation (www.bmtcinfo.com) handles all the buses plying within the city, and the Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation (www.ksrtc.in) operates buses within the state as well as outside the state. Other State Road Transport Corporations operate buses outside the state.
Nearest inter-state bus stands: KempeGowda Bus Station (Majestic) Nearest intra city bus stands: Majestic (Route # 271, 271 D, 273, 276), Shivaji Nagar (Route # 272), Yeshwantpur (No direct buses), Hebbal (Route # 500) Nearest bus stop: Jalahalli Village/ M.E.S Ring Road/ BEL Circle. A number of private agencies also run bus services to cities and towns across the country. These buses mainly operate from Kalasipalayam near city market.
Rail:
There are three railway stations near to the church. They are Bangalore City (Majestic), Cantonment (Shivaji Nagar) and Yeshwanthpur. You can find more information at www.indianrail.gov.in
Air:
Bangalore is well-connected to all major cities in India and abroad by air. The Bangalore International Airport (www.bengaluruairport.com )is located at Devanahalli near to city. If you use public transport system from airport, reach Hebbal using Airport busses and proceed towards BEL Circle.
